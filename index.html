Become a SpaceX Rocket Flight Pilot

Introduction

As SpaceX continues to expand its ambitious intercontinental rocket flight program, we are looking for highly skilled and dedicated pilots to join our team. This document provides an overview of the SpaceX Pilot Recruitment Program, outlining the job requirements, necessary certifications, and the application process. The information provided here will serve as a foundation for students to create a user-friendly web flow for prospective pilots to learn about the opportunity and sign up to become a SpaceX pilot.
